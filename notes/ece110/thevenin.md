## Thévenin & Norton Equivalents

### Key Idea

Any linear circuit can be reduced to $V_{th}$ in series with $R_{th}$.

### Steps

- Remove the load. Find $V_{oc}$ → this is $V_{th}$
- Kill sources, find $R_{eq}$ → this is $R_{th}$
- Norton conversion: $I_N = \frac{V_{th}}{R_{th}}$

### Formula

$$I_D = \frac{k_n}{2}(V_{GS} - V_{tn})^2$$
